96 years of average monthly temperatures from Bear Island, a Norwegian island in the Barents Sea.

Below 12 bar plots show the average temperatures scaled by month. Each value represents the number of standard deviations the temperature was above or below the mean for that month over 96 years of observations.

A trend of higher average monthly temperatures emerges for some months. This trend is most notable for April and May.
