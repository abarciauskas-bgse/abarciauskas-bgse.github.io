Principal Components of Red Wine Quality

Mar 6, 2016

Below is a biplot of the first 2 prinicipal components of the Wine Quality dataset.

High quality wines have a greater levels of alcohol, sulfates and citric acid, and lower levels of volatile acidity, total and free sulfur dioxide dimensions.
